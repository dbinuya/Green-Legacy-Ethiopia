Ethiopian Green Legacy

Ethiopia's Green Legacy initiative aims to combat climate change and environmental degradation through massive reforestation efforts Launched in 2019, it targets planting billions of trees annually to
restore degraded lands and enhance biodiversity. This ambitious
project seeks to improve air quality, support local communities,
and mitigate the impacts of global warming, positioning Ethiopia as a leader in sustainable environmental stewardship. click here

The Green Legacy Initiative in Ethiopia represents a profound commitment to environmental sustainability and climate resilience. By ambitiously planting billions of trees, the initiative not only aims to restore degraded landscapes and enhance biodiversity but also serves as a model for global reforestation efforts. Through these actions, Ethiopia is making significant strides in combating climate change, improving local livelihoods, and fostering a healthier planet. The initiative underscores the critical role that dedicated environmental stewardship plays in building a sustainable future for all.😃
